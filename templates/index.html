About This Project

TriageXpert is an advanced demonstration of how AI can assist in the initial stages of healthcare. It uses Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models (like Google's Gemini) to analyze symptoms and provide structured, helpful information. This tool is not a substitute for a doctor but aims to empower users with better information to seek appropriate care in a timely manner.
